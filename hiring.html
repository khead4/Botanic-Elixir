Careers at Botanic Elixir

Botanic Elixir is building a hospitality team for an adaptive cafe where drinks, booking flows, room cues, and human care help guests choose the right state for focus, play, connection, or restoration.

Open roles

Pick the role that fits you. If you’re hybrid, apply anyway — we love overlap.


Guest Experience Lead

Hospitality + Bookings

You welcome guests, manage the floor rhythm, support bookings, and help people choose the experience, drink, or room setup that fits their intention. The role blends reception, community hosting, and calm real-time coordination.


You’ll do

  • Welcome guests and guide check-ins, seating, and transitions
  • Recommend experiences, menu pairings, and room options
  • Coordinate bookings, wait times, and small-group flow
  • Keep the space calm, clean, warm, and easy to navigate
You’re great if

  • You have hospitality, events, reception, or community experience
  • You read people well and communicate with warmth
  • You stay organized when the room gets busy
  • You can make people feel oriented without overexplaining

Botanical Bar Specialist

Drinks + Prep

You prepare the botanical drinks and light food menu with consistency, care, ingredient awareness, and enough pace to support a real service.

You’ll do

  • Prepare signature drinks, teas, tonics, and small plates
  • Maintain quality, speed, cleanliness, and stock rotation
  • Track allergens, substitutions, and daily prep needs
  • Support “pairing” moments (drink + intention)
You’re great if

  • You have barista, prep kitchen, cafe, or wellness-bar experience
  • You care about presentation and consistency
  • You understand allergens and ingredient safety
  • You can move quickly without losing attention to detail

Experience Systems Technician

AV + Digital Ops

You maintain the digital and physical systems that support the experience: booking screens, simple automations, audio/visual cues, projection, lighting, and troubleshooting.


You’ll do

  • Maintain booking, menu, and checkout flows for the venue team
  • Support AV, projection, audio, lighting cues, and troubleshooting
  • Prototype lightweight interactive features and signage updates
  • Document systems so the team can operate confidently
You’re great if

  • You have practical AV, venue tech, front-end, or operations experience
  • You can explain technical issues in calm, simple language
  • You enjoy practical prototyping and fast fixes
  • You care about reliability because guests feel the difference
