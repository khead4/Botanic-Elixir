Privacy Policy

Botanic Elixir collects only the information needed to prepare bookings, requests, applications, and guest communications with care.

What We Collect

We may collect your name, email, phone number, booking details, menu selections, consultation notes, application details, and messages you choose to send through the site.

How We Use It

We use this information to prepare your visit, save confirmations in your browser, respond to inquiries, improve the guest experience, and keep operational records for service quality.

Storage

Some confirmations and bag details are stored locally in your browser so the experience can work without forcing account creation. If you email us, your message is handled through your email provider and our team inbox.

Your Choices

  • You can clear local browser storage to remove saved bag, booking, or application details from this device.
  • You can contact us to update or remove information you sent directly to the team.
  • You can choose not to provide optional information in forms.

Contact

For privacy questions, email hello@botanicelixir.com.
