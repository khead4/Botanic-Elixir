Using The Site

The site is provided to explore Botanic Elixir, request experiences, build a bag, prepare inquiries, and contact the team. Please provide accurate information when submitting forms.

Bookings

Booking requests and confirmations may be saved in your browser and/or prepared for email confirmation. Times, group sizes, and availability may be adjusted by the team before a visit is final.

Orders

Menu items and bag totals shown on the site are for planning and checkout flow demonstration. Final availability, substitutions, allergens, and pricing should be confirmed with the team before service.

Community Conduct

  • Respect the quiet, social, and restorative zones of the space.
  • Share accurate accessibility, allergy, and timing needs when relevant.
  • Use community events and inquiries in a respectful, non-disruptive way.

Contact

For questions about bookings, orders, or terms, email hello@botanicelixir.com.
