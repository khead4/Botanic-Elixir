About Our Ingredients


All serums are handcrafted in-house using organic, sustainably-sourced ingredients. We work directly with herbalists and suppliers who share our commitment to quality and ethical practices. Each ingredient is third-party tested for purity and potency. We never use artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives. All our offerings are 100% non-alcoholic, vegan, and gluten-free.


Please inform staff of any allergies or sensitivities. Individual responses to botanicals may vary.
